The Rules of the Game.

Become A Legend In 90 Secs is a World Cup trivia game, created to give you the chance to the become your own version of a World Cup legend.

In 90 seconds, answer a variety of World Cup related questions, and you stand a chance to earn bragging rights, top the Wordl XI leaderboard, and win a consolation gift. It is free to play, and only depends on how many times you can become a legend.
